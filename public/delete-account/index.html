Delete Your Account

You can permanently delete your Calmisu account using the options below.

This action is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be removed.

What will be deleted

  • Your profile (name, email address)
  • Activity history and streaks
  • Chat history stored on your device
  • Subscription information

Active subscriptions are not automatically refunded. Contact your app store for refunds.

Option 1 — Delete in the app

  1. Open the Calmisu app and sign in.
  2. Go to Profile → Settings.
  3. Tap Delete Account and confirm.

Option 2 — Request by email

If you cannot access the app, send a deletion request from the email address linked to your account to calmisuapp@gmail.com. We will process it within 30 days.
